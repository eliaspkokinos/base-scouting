The framework

The B.A.S.E. Framework

Data and film tell you what happened. They don't tell you why, or whether it'll happen somewhere else. The framework combines statistical analysis, data visualizations, and observed behaviors to separate what a player genuinely is from what their environment is letting them look like.

Every report covers observed behaviors, Player DNA classification, league transferability across seven target leagues, and a four-axis risk assessment — with data visualizations built to make the picture clear, not just complete.

Read the full methodology

Pure Traits — Will Travel

Survives system changes and league transitions. Not environment-protected.

System Traits — Environment Dependent

Output drops significantly outside specific tactical setups or club conditions.

Context Traits — Situation Dependent

More effective in specific game states — not reliable across all phases.

Exposed Traits — Will Be Targeted

Known weaknesses that better players and coaches will find and exploit.

The territory

One region.
Untapped talent.

The Balkans have a deep footballing heritage and a proven track record. Serbia, Croatia, Greece, Romania, Bosnia — these leagues keep producing technically gifted players that major European clubs are only now starting to look at seriously.

B.A.S.E. covers 12 countries across the region — from the Adriatic coast to the Black Sea — with a focus on players aged 16–24 who have the tools to succeed at higher levels but haven't yet had the platform to prove it.
