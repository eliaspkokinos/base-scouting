Role definition
Defensive Fullback
Disciplined fullbacks who prioritise defensive solidity, 1v1 duels, and maintaining the back line's shape over attacking contribution. Their value is in what they prevent, not what they create.
Similar archetypes
César Azpilicueta, Aaron Wan-Bissaka, Danilo
Role definition
Attacking Fullback
High-energy fullbacks who overlap frequently, stretch the pitch vertically, and contribute consistently in the final third. The attacking output is the point — they're essentially a third winger in possession.
Similar archetypes
Achraf Hakimi, Alphonso Davies, Jeremie Frimpong
Role definition
Playmaking Fullback
Technically gifted fullbacks who step into midfield to dictate tempo and progression. Less about beating their man in wide areas, more about breaking lines with the pass. The modern game's most tactically nuanced fullback type.
Similar archetypes
João Cancelo, Oleksandr Zinchenko, Trent Alexander-Arnold
Role definition
Complete Fullback
Balanced fullbacks who impact both ends of the pitch — reliable defensively, intelligent in overlaps, and capable of quality service into the box. The hardest fullback profile to find because it demands consistency in every phase.
Similar archetypes
Theo Hernández, Reece James, Benjamin Pavard
Role definition
No-Nonsense Centerback
Old-school centerbacks who prioritise clearances, blocks, and physical dominance. They offer security through simplicity — they don't try to play out from the back when the right answer is to clear their lines.
Similar archetypes
James Tarkowski, Nemanja Vidić, Diego Godín
Role definition
Ball-Playing Centerback
Technically confident centerbacks comfortable under pressure — able to break lines with accurate passing and support buildup from deep. The type modern possession-based coaches specifically recruit for.
Similar archetypes
Aymeric Laporte, Mats Hummels, Alessandro Bastoni
Role definition
Complete Centerback
All-around centerbacks with the tools to defend in space, duel physically, and contribute to buildup — equally comfortable in a high or low block. The complete package, and the ceiling for what a centerback can be.
Similar archetypes
Virgil van Dijk, Nico Schlotterbeck, Joško Gvardiol
